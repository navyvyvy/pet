# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a lightweight desktop pixel pet utility and already have PNG frames or a spritesheet they are allowed to use. They configure it while doing normal desktop work and expect the app to stay out of the way.

## Product Purpose

PetPlayer lets users load their own PNG assets, preview frame mapping, save local settings, and run a small animated pet bar from the system tray.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, friendly.

## Anti-references

No copyrighted sample characters, no game or animation franchise references, no decorative dashboard styling, and no busy toy-like settings UI.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the pet bar invisible except for the pets.
2. Make asset setup direct and inspectable.
3. Preserve user choices across restarts.
4. Fail with placeholders instead of crashes.
5. Use restrained product controls that feel familiar on desktop.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and reduced-motion alternatives for UI transitions. The pet animation can pause from the tray.
